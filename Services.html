Bhubaneswar Ajay Padhi - Co-Founder -

Founder

Since childhood he was very much interested in films. At the very early age, he aspired to become a director instead of an actor. After completing his education in Cinematography in 2013, he has presented some commercially successful films. He has also worked with many talented directors like Imtiaz Ali, Tinu Suresh Desai and Arup Adhikari and directed some commercial advertisements and serials. Currently he is one of the members of IFTDA (Indian film and television director association).
